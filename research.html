Ph.D. Thesis

Li Xinyi. (2024). Exploring Disjunctions in Chinese: A Comprehensive Analysis of Distinctions, Usage, and Historical Development. Doctoral thesis, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

This thesis provides a comprehensive analysis of disjunctive constructions in Chinese, examining the semantic distinctions between major disjunction types, their contextual usage patterns, and the historical pathways through which disjunction markers developed in Sinitic languages.
